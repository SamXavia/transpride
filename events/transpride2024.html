Trans Pride Southampton 2024

We are returning for our Third Annual Trans Pride Southampton event on June 29th 2024. This time we are reutrning to our Year One venue; Edmund Kell Unitarian Church. We will be kicking the event off by having a protest march from the London Hotel starting from 12pm (Noon) and will be marching all through Southampton all the way to Edmund Kell for 1pm door opening time.

FAQ

Q: Do you have an accessability point for the March? A: Yes we do, it will be outside HMV near bargate.
Q: How long is the event? A: We have no set end time as we have a disco in the evening starting from 7pm
Q: Do I have to be Trans to attend? A: Not at all, as long as you are respectable, anyone is welcome including Straight Cis people.
